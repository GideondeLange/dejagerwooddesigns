Our Services

Epoxy Resin Tables

Our flagship service. We design and pour custom epoxy resin tables in any colour, size, and wood species. River tables, ocean tables, geode tables, full-pour slabs — all built to your exact specification.

  • Any epoxy colour or combination
  • Dining, coffee, desk and side table sizes
  • Various wood species available
  • Fully sealed and polished finish
  • Steel, wood or resin leg options
🪚

Saw Mill Services

Have your own logs? Bring them to us and we'll mill them into usable lumber. Perfect for farmers, builders, and anyone wanting to make use of their own timber.

  • Max log length: 4.2m
  • Max log width: 1.1m
  • Cut to any thickness
  • Slabs, planks, beams available
Max: 4.2m × 1.1m
🌿

Live Edge Slabs

Natural raw wood slabs with preserved live edges, sourced and dried in-house. Available as-is for DIY projects, or finished into furniture by us.

  • Multiple species in stock
  • Natural edge fully preserved
  • Oiled or resin-finished options
  • Available raw for DIY
🔪

Cutting Boards

Handcrafted end-grain and face-grain cutting boards — beautiful enough to display, durable enough for daily use. Great as gifts or functional kitchen pieces.

  • End-grain & face-grain options
  • Custom sizes and shapes
  • Food-safe mineral oil finish
  • Personalised engraving available
📐

Custom Wood Cutting

Need wood cut to specific dimensions? We cut timber to your exact measurements for a perfect fit — whether for shelving, furniture builds, or construction.

  • Cut to your exact dimensions
  • Various species and thicknesses
  • Straight, angled, and shaped cuts
  • Ready to use — sanded and clean
