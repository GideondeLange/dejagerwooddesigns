How to Order

Ordering a custom epoxy table in South Africa doesn't need to be complicated. We walk you through the whole process — from the first conversation to the moment we deliver your finished piece.

The Process

From Conversation to Delivery

💬

1. The Conversation

Tell us what you're imagining — dimensions, the room it'll live in, whether you prefer a river table, a solid slab, a live edge piece, or something else entirely. We listen and ask the right questions.

🪚

2. Slab Selection

We select your slab from our Benoni saw mill — you're welcome to come in and choose it yourself. We agree on your epoxy colour, finish and leg style, then build begins.

🎨

3. The Build

Each piece is handcrafted from start to finish — precision cutting, professional epoxy pour, grinding, and a hand-polished finish. Most pieces take 4–8 weeks from deposit to delivery.

🚚

4. Delivery & Install

We deliver and install across Gauteng. Transport to other provinces can be arranged on request. We don't hand over until you're happy with the result.

Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a custom table take?

Most pieces take between 4 and 8 weeks from deposit to delivery, depending on complexity, slab drying time, and current order queue.

Is a deposit required?

Yes — we require a deposit to secure your slot and purchase materials. The balance is due on completion before delivery.

Can I visit the workshop?

Absolutely. You're welcome to come in, choose your slab in person, and see work in progress. Just WhatsApp us to arrange a visit.

Do you deliver outside Gauteng?

We deliver and install across Gauteng. For other provinces, we can arrange transport with a reputable courier — just ask when you enquire.

How do I care for my epoxy table?

Wipe with a damp cloth. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight. Use coasters for hot items. Our tables are sealed with a UV-resistant top coat for long-term durability.

Can I choose the wood and colour?

Yes — 100%. You choose the species, the epoxy colour, the finish (matte or gloss), and the leg style. Every piece is fully custom.
