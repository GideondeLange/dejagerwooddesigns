Where Every Table Starts

The De Jager saw mill in Benoni is where everything starts. Raw logs come in, furniture-grade slabs go out — thick, wide, and already vetted by the same hands that will build your table. This is what separates a De Jager piece from anything you'll find in a showroom or catalogue.

Our saw mill on the East Rand carries a rotating selection of South African hardwood slabs including Saligna, Kiaat (African Teak), Red Iron Bark, Rosewood and White Oak — available in a wide range of lengths, widths and thicknesses to suit dining tables, coffee tables, bar counters and full countertop runs. We also take in customer-supplied logs and mill them to your specified dimensions.

Not sure what you need? Come visit the mill in Northmead, Benoni — walk the floor, feel the timber, and we'll talk through your project together. No obligation, no pressure.

Species We Mill & Stock

Saligna

Dense and warm-toned with a tight, consistent grain. Our most popular species for river tables across Gauteng — it holds epoxy beautifully and produces wide, stable slabs that are ideal for dining table builds.

🟥

Kiaat (African Teak)

Rich reddish-brown with a deep, pronounced grain pattern. Kiaat is one of the most sought-after South African hardwoods for furniture — incredibly durable, naturally oil-rich, and stunning with any dark epoxy colour.

Red Iron Bark, Rosewood & White Oak

Premium hardwoods for discerning builds. Red Iron Bark is exceptionally dense and earthy, Rosewood brings deep chocolate tones, and White Oak offers a versatile blonde grain that pairs with any epoxy colour.

Have timber on your property? We'll turn it into something useful. Farmers, builders and homeowners bring us their logs — we mill them to your exact specifications and hand them back ready to use.

Milling Specifications

We handle large-format logs with ease — from a single backyard tree to a full farm clearing.

  • Max log width: 1.1 metres
