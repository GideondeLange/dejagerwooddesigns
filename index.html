Our Story

Crafted with Passion.
Built to Last.

Based in Benoni on the East Rand, De Jager Wood Designs crafts custom epoxy resin tables built to last a lifetime and look better every year. Whether you're after a river table for your Johannesburg dining room, an epoxy resin countertop for your kitchen, or a statement live edge coffee table for your lounge — we build it from scratch, for you.

Every table starts with a slab of real South African hardwood — Kiaat, Saligna, Rosewood or other premium hardwoods — selected by hand from our own saw mill in Benoni. The wood is paired with premium epoxy resin in any colour you choose, poured and finished to a glass-smooth surface that you'll be proud to show off for decades.

Saw Mill · Benoni

We Cut Our Own Wood.

Most furniture makers buy their timber from a supplier. We don't. Our saw mill in Northmead, Benoni processes raw logs into furniture-grade hardwood slabs — Kiaat, Saligna, Tamboti and more — that go straight into your table. No middlemen, no markups, no mystery timber.

We also sell slabs direct to woodworkers, furniture makers and DIY enthusiasts across Gauteng. If you need quality hardwood slabs on the East Rand, come visit the mill — stock rotates regularly and every piece has passed our own quality check.
